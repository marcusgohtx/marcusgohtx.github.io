Some planning tools are optimized for storing information, but not for shaping a schedule quickly.

`schedular` started from a more specific need: laying out events across a few days, moving pieces around visually, and then exporting the result into a calendar format that other tools can understand.

The goal is not to become a giant project-management suite. The goal is to make one high-friction task feel easy:

1. sketch the plan
2. drag it into shape
3. export it cleanly

That narrow scope is what makes the tool feel useful.
